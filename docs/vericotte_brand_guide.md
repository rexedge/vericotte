## Our Logo

### Primary Logotype

The primary logotype is the go-to mark for all brand communications. It is a trademark to help viewers easily identify the Vericotte brand. It is essential that the logo is always applied with care and respect.

The full primary lockup consists of:
- The Vericotte icon mark (parallelogram crystal shape in teal and orange)
- The wordmark "Vericotte" in the logo typeface
- The orange dot on the letter "i" — **never remove or recolor this dot**

### Alternative Lookups

| Variant | Use Case |
|---|---|
| Typographic-only mark | Small screen sizes where the icon reduces legibility |
| Icon mark only | App icons, favicons, embossed applications |
| Full information lockup | Forms, email signatures, official documents |

**Information lockup format:**
```
[Logo Icon] Vericotte
12345 Creigh Williems Street, Suite 607, Lagos to Badagry Exp. way
vericotte.com
```

### Black & White Versions

- **On dark backgrounds:** Inverse the logo to full white
- **When color is unavailable:** Use all-black version
- Never mix black and colored elements in a single-color application

### Logo Clear Space

Always surround the logo with clear space equal to the height of the capital **V** in the wordmark. No other graphic elements, typography, rules, or images may appear inside this clear space.

### Correct Logo Usage

The logo may be placed on:
- White background
- Black background
- Gray background
- Any color from the primary or secondary brand palette
- Photography — only where sufficient contrast exists; use white logotype on imagery in most cases

### Incorrect Logo Usage — Do Not:

1. Change the colors of the logo
2. Place elements inside the logo clear space
3. Condense, expand, or distort the logo disproportionately
4. Add drop shadows, bevel/emboss, inner glow, or any text effects
5. Adjust the placement of the logo icon relative to the wordmark
6. Place the logo on imagery with poor contrast or readability
7. Resize individual elements of the logo independently
8. Rotate the logo or the typography
9. Crop the logo
10. Remove the crystal shape or the dot on the letter "i"
11. Change the color of the dot on "i" — except when applying a single-color version of the full logo

## Brand Typeface

### Logo Typeface

**Commune** is used exclusively within the logo wordmark.

```
ABCDEFGHIJKLMNOPQRSTUVWXYZ
abcdefghijklmnopqrstuvwxyz
(.,;:?!@#$%^&*) 0123456789
```

### Primary Typeface — Montserrat

Montserrat is the primary brand typeface and should be used for all headings, subheadings, and key messaging. It is a web-safe font with a wide range of weights.

```
ABCDEFGHIJKLMNOPQRSTUVWXYZ
abcdefghijklmnopqrstuvwxyz
(.,;:?!@#$%^&*) 0123456789
```

**Available weights:** Light · Regular · Medium · SemiBold · Bold · ExtraBold

### Secondary Typeface — Omnes

Omnes serves as the supporting typeface for body copy and alternate subheading styles.

### Fallback Typeface — Verdana

Use Verdana when Montserrat and Omnes are unavailable and a standard system font is required.

### Typographic Hierarchy

| Element | Font | Weight | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Display / Hero | Montserrat | ExtraBold 800 | Large headlines |
| Section Headings | Montserrat | Bold 700 | Major section titles |
| Subheadings | Montserrat | SemiBold 600 | Sub-section labels |
| Body Copy | Omnes / Montserrat | Regular 400 | Paragraphs |
| Captions / Labels | Montserrat | Medium 500 | Small text, tags |

## Graphic Patterns

The Vericotte icon pattern — based on the parallelogram crystal shape from the logo — can be used across a variety of brand collateral. The pattern may appear:
- As a full background texture
- Tinted or at reduced opacity
- With type or imagery placed on top

**Pattern color variants:**
- Orange on orange (tonal)
- Light gray on white
- Teal on dark teal
- Cyan on cyan
- Dark charcoal on dark navy
- Deep navy on deep navy

## Brand Stationery

### Business Card

**Front:**
- Crystal logo icon + city photography (Lagos skyline) in background
- Staff name in bold Montserrat
- Job title in caps, small, muted
- Contact details (phone, email, website, address) with brand-colored icons

**Back:**
- Full Vericotte logotype centered
- Tagline: *Navigating Complexity, Innovating Tomorrow*
- Lagos city skyline image as background (tinted)

### ID Card

- Dark navy background (`#153241`)
- White Vericotte logo (top-left)
- Staff photo (centered, white-bordered frame)
- Divider line in brand orange
- Name in bold white Montserrat
- Job title in uppercase, muted
- Contact details with orange icon circles
- QR code (bottom-right)
- Orange lanyard with repeating Vericotte wordmark

### Letterhead

- Vericotte logo (top-right or top-center)
- Tagline: *Navigating Complexity, Innovating Tomorrow*
- Footer bar with contact details: phone · email · website · address
- Body area in white with generous margins
